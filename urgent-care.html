Urgent Care Centers

Jefferson Health / Urgent Care Centers

When your condition can't wait – like a bad cough or a sprained knee – Jefferson Health Urgent Care is your best option.

You feel ill or have a minor injury. Your doctor's office either has closed for the day or can't squeeze you into the schedule. You think about going to the emergency room, but your condition isn't life threatening and you don't want to sit in the waiting room for hours.

Services

You will receive the same level of expert care as you've come to expect at Jefferson. Our Urgent Care doctors are highly trained and board certified in Emergency Medicine.

Conditions Treated

  • General illness
  • Seasonal allergies
  • Migraines
  • Skin rashes
  • Abscesses
  • Sprains and fractures
  • Cold, cough & flu symptoms
  • Minor burns

Services Offered

  • Care Coordination
  • Electrocardiograms
  • Intravenous Fluids
  • Nebulizer Therapy
  • On-site X-rays
  • On-site Lab
  • Physicals
  • Vaccinations (where available)

Our Urgent Care centers are available at many convenient locations.
