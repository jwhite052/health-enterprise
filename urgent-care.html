Urgent Care

Jefferson Health > Urgent Care

When your condition can't wait – like a bad cough or a sprained knee – Jefferson Health Urgent Care is your best option

You feel ill or have a minor injury. Your doctor's office either has closed for the day or can't squeeze you into the schedule. You think about going to the emergency room, but your condition isn't life threatening and you don't want to sit in the waiting room for hours.

We treat patients of all ages, from infants to seniors. No appointment necessary, walk-ins are welcome. On average, patients are treated and discharged within an hour.
