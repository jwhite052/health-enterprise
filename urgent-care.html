Urgent Care

Jefferson Health > Urgent Care

When your condition can't wait – like a bad cough or a sprained knee – Jefferson Health Urgent Care is your best option.

You feel ill or have a minor injury. Your doctor's office either has closed for the day or can't squeeze you into the schedule. You think about going to the emergency room, but your condition isn't life threatening and you don't want to sit in the waiting room for hours.

We're proud to say that the Jefferson at Washington Square urgent care and Smylie Times has been awarded the Certified Urgent Care Category 1 designation by the Urgent Care Association of America and are one of the select few facilities around the county that have met all of the UCAO's nationally-standardized criteria.

Know Before You Go
Have an unexpected illness or injury but unsure whether to rush to the ER? Knowing the difference between emergency care and urgent care can save your life. When in doubt, it's better to be safe and call 9-1-1 or go to the closest ER.

We treat patients of all ages, from infants to seniors. Walk-ins are welcome. On average, patients are treated and discharged within an hour.
