Our goal is to minimize pain, promote wellness and regain function – providing the highest quality of care for every patient, every day.

In Center City, we offer Jefferson Outpatient Imaging – a world-class outpatient diagnostic imaging facility integrating leading-edge medical technology with your individual needs in mind. Just north of the city, we have five outpatient imaging locations from Abington to Blue Bell, Pennsylvania. In New Jersey, we’ve got you covered with four outpatient imaging locations across Gloucester and Camden counties.
