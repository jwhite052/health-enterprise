Rehab Specialty Services

Jefferson Health > Rehab Specialty Services

JeffFit Outpatient Rehabilitation Services is dedicated to improving the quality of life by providing a comprehensive, team-based approach to physical therapy, occupational therapy and speech language pathology for adults and children at many locations including JeffFit-Jefferson, Abington Jefferson Health and Aria Jefferson Health.

At JeffFit, our goals are to minimize your pain, promote wellness and help you regain function, so you can get back to your everyday life. This is achieved by providing comprehensive patient evaluation and treatment, educating patients and families, and consulting with other healthcare providers. Patients receive personalized treatments in a comfortable setting, where they are able to build relationships with the dedicated staff.

Our staff includes therapists with specific expertise in various clinical specialties. The core values of our organization and clinical expertise demonstrate the staff’s firm commitment to providing the highest quality of care for every patient, every day.
