Jefferson Health Hospitals

Jefferson Health / Hospitals

Services close to home. Access to a broad physician network. And caregivers who care – about you.

Jefferson Health offers excellent clinical and compassionate care for our patients in the Philadelphia region, educating the health professionals of tomorrow in a variety of disciplines and discovering new knowledge that will define the future of clinical care.

Jefferson ranks as 16th Best Hospital on U.S. News & World Report's Honor Roll list. We continue to top the list of hospitals in Pennsylvania (3rd) and the Philadelphia metro area (2nd) in U.S. News & World Report's annual listing of the best hospitals and specialties.

Jefferson also stands out as among the best in the following specialty areas:

  • Orthopedics
  • Cancer
  • Ear, Nose & Throat
  • Gastroenterology & GI Surgery
  • Nephrology
  • Neurology & Neurosurgery
  • Ophthalmology
  • Diabetes & Endocrinology
  • Geriatrics
  • Cardiology & Heart Surgery
  • Urology
  • And more...
